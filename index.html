Your Dreams Are Possible

As a little girl I had dreams of being a college athlete and working my way to becoming a professional. I was motivated by the will to be the best.

In order to do so, I have had to treat every aspect of my life as an investment. I have had to invest in taking care of my body, I have had to invest a lot of time away from friends and hanging out.

Every second, every minute, every hour consists of living, sleeping, and eating basketball. The road has not been easy, but anything worth having is not easy. I am motivated knowing that I am one step closer to my dream.
